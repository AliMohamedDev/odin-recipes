Chow Mein

Savory Stir-Fried Chow Mein Delight

Chow Mein is a beloved Chinese stir-fried noodle dish, renowned for its flavorful combination of tender noodles, crisp vegetables, and succulent proteins like chicken, beef, shrimp, or tofu. This dish, enhanced with the rich taste of soy sauce, often includes vibrant ingredients such as bell peppers, onions, and bean sprouts. Perfect for a quick yet satisfying meal, Chow Mein captivates with its delightful blend of textures and savory flavors.

Ingredients

  • 14 oz yakisoba refrigerated noodles , or 16 oz chow mein noodles*
  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • salt and pepper
  • 3 Tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 cups finely shredded cabbage
  • 1 large carrot , shredded
  • 2 ribs celery , chopped
  • 4 green onions , chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic , minced
  • 2 teaspoons freshly grated ginger
  • sauces

Steps

  1. Cook the noddles
  2. Prepare the sauce
  3. Cook the protein
  4. Stir-fry the vegtables
  5. Combine ingredients
  6. Serve
